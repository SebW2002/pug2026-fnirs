01 Overview

After our first hands-on projects in March and April, we, the Student Network for Neuroscience as part of the PsyFaKo e.V., are proud to announce our second "hands-on" project “fNIRS with NIRx”. Together with NIRx Medizintechnik GmbH and FSI Psychology of HU Berlin (FSI), we’re excited to invite you to this series focusing on the fundamentals of fNIRS application, data collection and analysis. Toward the end of September / start of October we will offer our first next Workshops, focussing on Functional Near-Infrared Spectroscopy! There will be a Hands-On, In-Person Workshop taking place in Berlin where the fundamental skills on how to measure fNIRS will be taught, as well as an Online Hackathon, in which Data Processing and Analysis will be covered!
